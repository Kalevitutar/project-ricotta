Welcome to Project Ricotta!

What is Project Ricotta?

I'm glad you asked! Project Ricotta is a hackathon combining the delicious talents of Lasagna Love with the girl-techie know-how of Girl Develop It.


What is Girl Develop It?

Girl Develop It (GDI) is a national nonprofit organization that creates welcoming, supportive opportunities for women and non-binary adults to learn software development skills. Find out more at Girl Develop It


What is a hackathon?

A hackathon is when a bunch of talented and eager techies get together over a short period of time, such as a weekend, to create something together. Kinda like flash mobs, but on the internet.
